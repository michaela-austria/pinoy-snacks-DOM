Features

Order traditional Filipino snacks within the app and avoid long lines.

Available kiosk in our branches.

Kiosks are available in all branches of Pinoy Snacks. Customers can order a variety of snacks and drinks, including rice meals.

Access the kiosk on your own device.

To access the kiosk on your device. Just click the ‘Order Now’ button from our website, and you will be redirected to the kiosk.

operations

This is how you can order through Pinoy Snacks kiosk.

Ordering Process Simplified

Select your preferred food by taping the selection, update your order quantity.

Enter Cash

Once you have finished selecting your order, enter the cash amount that you will pay to the counter.

Claim Receipt

The receipt updates in real-time. Claim your receipt, and proceed to the counter and claim your order.
